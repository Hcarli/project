Comparing countries

In this section, using the raw data from penn world table to visualize and compare the evolution the indicators from two coutries. This is an primary form of comparison of countries.

The indicators get to penn world table to compare are:

  • Expenditure-side real GDP at chained PPPs (in mil. 2017US$)
  • Population (in millions)
  • Number of persons engaged (in millions)
  • Human capital index, based on years of schooling and returns to education
  • Capital stock at current PPPs (in mil. 2017US$)
  • TFP at constant national prices (2017=1)
  • Average depreciation rate of the capital stock
  • Share of gross capital formation at current PPPs

This indicators will be transform to indicator to use in Sollow Model soon.
